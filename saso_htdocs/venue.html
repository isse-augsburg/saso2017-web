Venue

The ICCAC and SASO conferences and the workshops will be held at The University of Arizona. For the hotel, we have a special agreement -- the Homewood Suites by Hilton® Tucson/St. Philip's Plaza, Tucson, AZ. Homewood Suites by Hilton is pleased to provide their award-winning hospitality and the following standard amenities:

  • Spacious one-bedroom suites with fully equipped kitchens
  • Complimentary Hot Breakfast Buffet daily and Social Reception featuring appetizers and beverages Monday- Wednesday evenings
  • Complimentary Shuttle to and from the UA Campus via our 9-Passenger Van
  • Complimentary WiFi in-Suite and throughout the Hotel
  • Complimentary Parking
  • Hilton Honors where guests earn both Points & Miles®
