Sponsoring

The IEEE International Conference on Self-Adaptive and Self-Organizing Systems is the premier international conference on self-adaptive, self-organizing systems taking place annually in venues around the world (http://www.saso-conference.org/). The conference usually attracts 100-150 participants, mainly from academia, government, and industry.

SASO'16 will come to Augsburg, Germany, in September 12-16, 2016. It will offer an extensive program that is of interest to academia, government, and industry. More than 100 experts from all over the world will get together for five days to exchange ideas and discuss about cutting-edge research and experiences gained in the inter-disciplinary field of self-* systems. You are crucial as an industry sponsor to make sure the conference has a practical grounding.

We want to make this a great event for all participants while showing the strength of software and hardware industries. This is a great chance for your company to talk to customers and future employees, improve your products’ visibility, expand your network, learn from competitors and partners, and initiate collaborations. All while getting up-to-date on the latest results for improving your practice.

There are a number of ways you can support and get involved in the event and help make this conference the best SASO conference ever. We offer many different sponsor packages, but there are also a number of exhibition spaces, events, and items to sponsor and brand with your logo. At SASO'16 in Augsburg, you can both get up to speed with the field and make yourself known to the community!

Become a Sponsor

There are several opportunities for your company/organization to be involved in SASO'16. By participating in this international event you will be able to exhibit your organization’s technical expertise and, at the same time, to have a look at the latest worldwide research and industrial trends.

Different sponsorship packages are available and if you are interested in participating please directly contact our team at saso2016@isse.de.
